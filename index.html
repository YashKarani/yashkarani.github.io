The thesis

You don't have a lead problem.
You have a system problem.

Most B2B teams treat outbound like a numbers game. Blast thousands of emails, hope for a few replies, call it a strategy. That isn't outreach. That's noise.

I build the opposite: signal-based systems where every list, every touch, and every follow-up is engineered to reach the right person at the right moment with a message they can't ignore. Over 5+ years and 700+ engagements, I've turned cold outbound into pipeline that founders can actually forecast.

What I engineer

Not campaigns. Pipeline infrastructure.

01

GTM Infrastructure
